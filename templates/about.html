What is Deepfakes?

Deepfake technology uses artificial intelligence to create hyper-realistic fake audio and video content, often making it appear as if someone is saying or doing something they never did. While this technology can have creative and legitimate uses, it also poses significant risks. Deepfakes can be used to spread misinformation, damage reputations, and manipulate public opinion, making it increasingly difficult to discern truth from deception in the digital age.
